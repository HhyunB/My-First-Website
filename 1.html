    Animal Crossing New Horizon

    Animal crossing New Horizon is a game made by Nintendo , released in 2020 Spring.

    It has been updated until November 05, 2021 with a lot of events. The events are different and it was based on the seasons.

    For instance, it has had a carnival events, Thanksgiving Events, New Year events, the bride of June events, etc. Those various events gives more items to play with, also the game itself becomes more flourious.
